I do love me some Johnny Cash. 
Some tips: go early or on off days. This place can get really crowded and that takes away from the museum. 
It's small and has no re entry. So make sure you see everything you want to before leaving. 
Listen to the music and watch the footage they have. It's pretty interesting. 

That being said I wish that they either picked a larger venue and gave people more room or allowed for you to come in and out over the weekend to see it with less people.  It is also a little spendy for what you're getting. But when in Nashville.....
