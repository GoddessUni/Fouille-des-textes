I love this spot. Definitely a few steps up from our typical Dive-Bars. Locally owned and operated this ambiance is some kind of wonderful! This place understands the value of good customer service. The staff is always cordial and management personally greets all customers. Complimentary specialty drinks to start the night always has a creative twist. The craft cocktails are made by a guru bartender with fresh fruit and other fixins.  The pimms cup is my fav! Guest appearances include Trombone Shorty and New Orleans Saints Players. The kitchen sits next to the bar which includes an array of pizzas and Gourmet popcorn. My favorite is the creole pizza and truffle pop corn. Always a good time at this place.
